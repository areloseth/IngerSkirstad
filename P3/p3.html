Changes made

We have made several small design changes throughout the developmentpart of this page. The main reason for this was the wishes of our client. Among these changes was the header, as Inger wanted text in this header. We also changed some aspects due to programming difficulties. We initially tried to use javascript to have a carousel of 3 images. As this proved difficult without the use of jquery we decided to go with a automatic slideshow. Another example of design change due to difficulty with programming was to have an order button in our modal. We tried to open our ordering-page with some info filled out in the url, but we could unfortunately not make it work.
