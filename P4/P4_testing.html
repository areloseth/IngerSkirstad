3. Tester Document

Google Form link. The questionnaire aimed to find out the age groups, platforms and browsers of our users, as well as their first impressions and the ease of use of our page.

4. Tester Audience

Our main tester where first and foremost our client. She has also been testing the site throughout the developing process, and her feedback has shaped our development. We have also let friends and family try our site. Among them some of Ingers previous customers

5. Testing Platforms

Most of our users browsed the page using Chrome on either a desktop or a laptop computer. We tested the page ourselves using Internet Explorer and Microsoft Edge and spotted no unwanted behaviors.

6. Feeback Summary

Navigation: Users found it very easy to navigate on the page. We recieved no feedback suggesting the oposite.

Content: Sadly, we were unable to test the order page which has a form that is to be sent to our client when someone places an order.

Design: The testers really liked the images used throughout the page as they felt they really captured the colours and style of ingers products. They also found the page to be really organized.
